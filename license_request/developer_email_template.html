To log into Confluence, you will find the "Authenticate by WSSO - Confluence" option in the drop down menu after you click on the in the upper left corner:

NOTE: You will not be able to use WSSO Log In with the Internet Explorer browser. Please use Firefox or Edge to access the SDTE.

Once logged on to Jira and Confluence, you should upload an email encryption certificate per the process outlined here:
Atlassian Email Encryption Setup
(You will need to do this for both Confluence and Jira)

NOTE: If you are a contractor, consultant, subsidiary, supplier, or customer and you are able to access Jira and Confluence but unable to access Bitbucket, you likely have filtered access to the Boeing Enterprise Network. Check your account in MARS. Select Account Search > Select the Search by Bemsid radio button > enter your BEMSID. In the Other column check the box next to SecureBadge Remote Access. Click Search. If your Filter status is "filtered", your sponsoring manager must submit a request to IT to grant you unfiltered access, based on the scope of contracted work. The Unfiltered/Transitive Access Request process is here. Please note, the Bitbucket servers will NOT be added to the Single Filter, skip that part where they ask you to put it on the single filter.
